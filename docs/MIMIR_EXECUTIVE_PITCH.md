## The Three Problems We Solve

### Problem 1: "The AI Forgot What We Decided Yesterday"

**What Happens Today:**
Your team asks an AI assistant for help on a complex project. Hour 1 goes great—research, planning, smart suggestions. By Hour 3, it's contradicting itself. By Hour 5, it's forgotten decisions from Hour 1. You end up redoing work or making mistakes.

**Why This Happens:**
AI assistants have working memory, like humans. Ask them to juggle 50 files and 20 decisions, and they lose track. Research from MIT proves even the newest AI models forget 40-60% of information when given too much context.

**How Mimir Fixes This:**
Instead of one overwhelmed AI, Mimir uses **specialized teams**:
- **PM AI:** Does research and creates a clear plan (stores it in a knowledge graph)
- **Worker AIs:** Each handles one piece, with just the info they need (95% less to remember)
- **QC AI:** Checks quality before anything goes to production

**Real Result:** Projects that used to fail at Hour 3 now run for days without forgetting a thing.

---

### Problem 2: "How Do We Trust AI With Important Work?"

**What Happens Today:**
AI makes mistakes—it invents facts, misunderstands requirements, or produces work that looks good but is wrong. Most companies either:
- Don't use AI for critical work (missing value)
- Use it and hope for the best (taking risks)

**Why This Happens:**
AI assistants optimize for speed, not accuracy. They're helpful but overconfident. There's no built-in "second check" before you act on their output.

**How Mimir Fixes This:**
We added **adversarial quality control**:
- Worker AIs try to complete tasks quickly
- QC AI tries to find problems (it's literally programmed to be skeptical)
- Nothing reaches production without passing QC
- Failed work gets specific feedback for a second attempt

**Real Result:** 95% of errors caught before they matter. Full audit trail for compliance. You can trust the output.

---

### Problem 3: "Our AI Tools Don't Talk to Each Other"

**What Happens Today:**
Your team uses AI for documentation, coding, customer service, and analysis—but each AI starts from scratch. They can't learn from each other or share knowledge. Work gets duplicated. Insights get lost.

**Why This Happens:**
Most AI tools have no memory between sessions. Even if they could remember, they don't have a common language or storage format.

**How Mimir Fixes This:**
We built a **permanent knowledge graph**:
- Every decision, file, task, and relationship gets stored
- New projects start with: "What have we learned about this before?"
- AIs can ask: "What depends on this?" or "Who worked on something similar?"
- Knowledge compounds over time instead of resetting

**Real Result:** Your AI team gets smarter with every project instead of starting over each time.
